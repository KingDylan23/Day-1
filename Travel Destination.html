Great destination for this Easter Holiday!!!

Mombasa is a very great place to go chill with your family this Easter Holiday.Mombasa was the first city in Kenya during the colonial era used mainly for trading due to it having a shore.

Some of the main attractions in Mombasa are:

  • The Indian Ocean shore makes it an ideal place to relax.
  • Fort Jesus Museum - Which was used for military fortification.
  • Mombasa Marine National parks and reserves.
  • Old town which is full of historical and cultural attractions.
  • Mamba Village Centre
